# Text Lock: Phone Call Record Management

Audience: business users considering whether kintone can manage phone call records and follow-up status.

Language for this prototype: English.

## Slide

- **Claim**: Turn every phone call into a shared record.
- **Support**: kintone keeps call details, follow-up status, and customer context in one place.
- **Speaker note**: Start from a familiar problem: phone calls are handled quickly, but the record, next action, and customer context often remain scattered.
- **Must keep**: phone call, shared record, follow-up status, kintone.
- **Route guidance**: B-hybrid should generate the phone-call scene and organized-management feeling as background only. Main copy and logo must stay editable PowerPoint overlays.

# Text Lock: kintone Field Feedback Loop

Audience: business users and managers evaluating whether kintone can reduce scattered field reporting and turn it into repeated improvement.

Language for this Route C test: English.

## 00_cover

- **Claim**: Turn scattered field feedback into one visible improvement loop.
- **Support**: Paper, spreadsheet, and chat-based updates are hard to compare. kintone can collect field input, approval status, notifications, and improvement actions in one shared work system.
- **Speaker note**: Open with a concrete operational loop rather than a feature list.
- **Must keep**: field feedback, improvement loop, kintone.

## 01_pain

- **Claim**: Separate updates hide the pattern.
- **Support**: A worker reports an issue, a manager checks a spreadsheet, and someone else follows up by message. Each piece exists, but the full trend is hard to see.
- **Speaker note**: The workflow makes learning difficult even when people are careful.
- **Must keep**: Separate updates hide the pattern.

## 02_solution

- **Claim**: One app connects input, approval, notice, and summary.
- **Support**: The same loop can show who entered the information, who approved it, which notification went out, and what changed in the summary view.
- **Speaker note**: Make the operating loop visible at a glance.
- **Must keep**: Input -> Approval -> Notice -> Summary.

## 03_continue

- **Claim**: Every small fix leaves a trace.
- **Support**: A visible history makes it easier to review, reuse, and expand successful changes across teams.
- **Speaker note**: Close by shifting from one issue to repeatable operational learning.
- **Must keep**: Small fixes leave a trace.

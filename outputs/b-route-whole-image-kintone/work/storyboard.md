# Storyboard: Whole-Image Route B Prototype

## 00_cover

- **Title**: kintone で業務改善を回し続ける
- **Primary job**: Define the deck as a visual story about continuous improvement.
- **Core claim**: kintone keeps improvement moving beyond the first implementation.
- **Support points**:
  - The story is about a working loop, not a feature list.
  - Use warm cybozu/kintone-like yellow and friendly work imagery.
  - The whole slide should read as one designed poster.
- **Visual metaphor**: A cheerful yellow cloud helper points to a circular improvement flow.
- **Speaker notes**: Open by saying this is a Route B image-first prototype. The slide is bitmap-final; notes are the editable layer.

## 01_pain

- **Title**: 散らばった仕事は、改善力を奪う
- **Primary job**: Make the pain visible before naming the solution.
- **Core claim**: Scattered work quietly drains the team's improvement capacity.
- **Support points**:
  - Paper, Excel, mail, and verbal checks are individually valid.
  - The problem is that the flow is invisible.
  - Show both field and management burden.
- **Visual metaphor**: A desk covered with papers, mails, spreadsheets, and confused workers.
- **Speaker notes**: Avoid blaming tools. Emphasize that disconnected flow creates repeated confirmation work.

## 02_loop

- **Title**: 入力、承認、通知、集計が同じデータの上で回る
- **Primary job**: Show the kintone-centered solution as a loop.
- **Core claim**: The same data can carry input, approval, notification, and aggregation.
- **Support points**:
  - kintone is the center of the operating loop.
  - People and information move around the same data.
  - Use the loop as the visual memory object.
- **Visual metaphor**: An orbit around a central yellow cloud with four labeled nodes.
- **Speaker notes**: Explain the loop as one data system rather than four disconnected features.

## 03_continue

- **Title**: 毎月見直すから、改善は止まらない
- **Primary job**: Land the story on operation after implementation.
- **Core claim**: Monthly review turns improvement into a habit.
- **Support points**:
  - Gather data.
  - Review what changed.
  - Update the next improvement point.
  - Repeat the loop.
- **Visual metaphor**: A calendar and circular arrows with review stamps and a helper cloud.
- **Speaker notes**: Close by saying the value is not just launch, but continuous operation.

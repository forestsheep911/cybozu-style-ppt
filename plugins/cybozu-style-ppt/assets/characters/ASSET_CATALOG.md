# Character Asset Catalog

Stable IDs for reusable transparent PNG character and scenario assets. Use the `id` when referencing an asset in prompts, scripts, or deck notes.

General rules:

- Use these as raster illustration assets inside editable PPT layouts.
- Keep labels, callouts, arrows, cards, and role chips as native PPT elements.
- Use one illustration family per slide.
- Prefer these assets over rough native-shape people drawings.
- Do not mix unrelated stock avatar styles, customer logo material, and kintone mascots on the same slide unless the story needs that contrast.
